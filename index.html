My Journey

In 2026, I committed to a path of radical transparency. I realized that true mastery comes from acquiring the indispensable skill of critical thinking within the markets.

Today, my mission is to share this roadmap with you. I distil complex trading practices into actionable insights, helping you navigate your own financial education.
